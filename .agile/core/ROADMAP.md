# divi Roadmap - Vision & Epics

## Vision
**divi** is a comprehensive, privacy-focused personal finance application. Our goal is to provide a "Mission Control" for personal finances, combining local-first performance with cloud-synced reliability.

---

## Epics

### [ ] Epic 1: Foundation & Core Dashboard (v0.2.0)
Establish the core architectural patterns and the first version of the financial dashboard with real data visualization.
- [ ] Connect dashboard cards to real store data.
- [ ] Implement historical patrimony calculation.
- [ ] Refine account carousel with real wallet data.

### [ ] Epic 2: Advanced Transaction Management (v0.3.0)
Enhanced tracking and categorization of financial movements.
- [ ] Multi-currency support foundation.
- [ ] Transaction recurring patterns.
- [ ] Advanced filtering and export capabilities.

### [ ] Epic 3: Budgeting & Goals (v0.4.0)
Proactive financial planning.
- [ ] Category-based monthly budgets.
- [ ] Savings goals with progress tracking.
- [ ] Automated alerts for budget overruns.

### [ ] Epic 4: Debt & Loan Management (v0.5.0)
Dedicated tools for managing liabilities.
- [ ] Amortization schedule visualization.
- [ ] Impact analysis of early repayments.

### [ ] Epic 5: Reports & Insights (v0.6.0)
Deep dive into financial health.
- [ ] Cash flow analysis.
- [ ] Spending heatmaps.
- [ ] AI-powered financial suggestions (local).

### [ ] Epic 6: PWA & Offline Optimization (v0.7.0)
Solidify the mobile-first, offline-ready experience.
- [ ] Background sync optimization.
- [ ] Biometric lock (Web Auth).
