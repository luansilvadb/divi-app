## 2025-05-19 - Removed high-contrast focus outlines in Sidebar **Aprendizado:** Interactive elements in sidebars benefit from subtle background transitions rather than sharp high-contrast outlines for a more integrated and aesthetically pleasing UI, especially when hover and active states already provide sufficient feedback. **Ação:** Removed `outline` and `outline-offset` from sidebar buttons and navigation items in `AppSidebar.vue`.
