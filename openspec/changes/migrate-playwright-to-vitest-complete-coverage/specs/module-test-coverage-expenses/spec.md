## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Expense services have complete test coverage
All expense tracking services SHALL be thoroughly tested.

#### Scenario: Expense recording is tested
- **WHEN** testing expense service
- **THEN** expense creation with all required fields SHALL be covered

#### Scenario: Expense categorization is tested
- **WHEN** testing expense categorization
- **THEN** category assignment and change SHALL be verified

#### Scenario: Recurring expenses are tested
- **WHEN** testing recurring expense handling
- **THEN** recurrence patterns and generation SHALL be covered

### Requirement: Expense UI components have test coverage
Expense-related UI components SHALL have comprehensive test coverage.

#### Scenario: Expense list is tested
- **WHEN** testing expense list view
- **THEN** display, sorting, and filtering SHALL be covered

#### Scenario: Expense entry form is tested
- **WHEN** testing expense form
- **THEN** quick entry, detailed entry, and validation SHALL be verified

#### Scenario: Expense analytics are tested
- **WHEN** testing expense chart components
- **THEN** data visualization and aggregation SHALL be accurate

### Requirement: Expense analysis is tested
Expense analysis and reporting functions SHALL be accurate.

#### Scenario: Spending trends calculation
- **WHEN** testing trend analysis
- **THEN** month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons SHALL be correct

#### Scenario: Top expense categories
- **WHEN** testing category aggregation
- **THEN** highest spending categories SHALL be correctly identified
