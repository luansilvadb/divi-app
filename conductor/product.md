# Product Guide: divi

## Vision
**divi** is a comprehensive, privacy-focused personal finance and budgeting application designed to empower individuals to take full control of their financial lives. By providing a suite of intuitive tools for tracking spending, managing budgets, setting financial goals, and monitoring debt, divi aims to make financial management accessible, insightful, and stress-free.

## Target Users
- **Budget-Conscious Individuals:** People looking for a structured way to track their income and expenses.
- **Goal-Oriented Savers:** Individuals working towards specific financial milestones (e.g., emergency fund, vacation, home purchase).
- **Debt Managers:** Users seeking to monitor and pay off loans and other liabilities.
- **Privacy-Minded Users:** Individuals who prefer to manage their financial data securely with modern web technologies.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive Dashboard:** A high-level overview of the user's financial health, including balances, recent activity, and progress towards goals.
- **Transaction Tracking:** Detailed logging and categorization of all financial transactions.
- **Budget Management:** Flexible tools for creating and monitoring monthly or category-specific budgets.
- **Goal Setting:** Ability to define financial goals and track progress over time.
- **Loan & Debt Monitoring:** Dedicated module for managing loans and understanding debt obligations.
- **Subscription Management:** Tracking recurring payments and subscriptions to identify potential savings.
- **Financial Reports:** Visualizations and summaries of spending habits, income trends, and net worth.
- **Calendar View:** A temporal view of upcoming bills, income, and scheduled financial events.
- **Activity Log:** A transparent record of changes and updates within the application.
- **Secure Authentication:** Robust user authentication and data protection powered by Supabase.
- **Local-First Architecture:** Engineered for maximum performance and privacy, storing data primarily on the user's device with IndexedDB (Dexie.js).
- **Progressive Web App (PWA):** Seamless access across devices with robust offline support and near-instant load times.
