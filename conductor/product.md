# Product Definition - divi

## Vision
A high-performance, cohesive, and logically dense finance management application focused on providing a seamless UI/UX using PrimeVue exclusively. The goal is to maximize developer productivity and application performance through strictly defined architectural patterns and modern tooling.

## Target Audience
- **Financial Enthusiasts:** Users who want detailed control over their budgets and transactions.
- **Productivity Seekers:** Users who value high information density and fast, responsive interfaces.

## Core Values
- **Performance:** Optimized bundle size and ultra-fast UI interactions.
- **Density:** Efficient use of screen real estate to provide maximum value with minimal scrolling.
- **Cohesion:** A unified look and feel powered by PrimeVue's design system.
- **Integrity:** Preservation of existing contracts and data reliability while modernizing the interface.

## Functional Scope
- **Asset/Wallet Management:** Tracking different types of accounts and assets.
- **Transaction Lifecycle:** Recording, categorizing, and analyzing financial movements.
- **Budgeting & Planning:** Proactive management of spending across categories.
- **Goal Tracking:** Visualizing progress towards long-term financial milestones.
- **Reporting:** Insightful charts and summaries of financial health.
- **Vertical Integration:** Modular features (Activity Log, Auth, Calendar, Loans, Subscriptions).
