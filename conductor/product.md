# Initial Concept
A personal finance and budget tracking application designed to help users manage their financial health.

## Target Audience
Individuals seeking comprehensive control over their personal finances, aiming to track expenses, set budgets, achieve financial goals, and monitor loans and subscriptions.

## Core Features
- **Dashboard & Activity Log:** A central hub providing an overview of financial status and recent activities.
- **Transaction Management:** Tracking daily income and expenses.
- **Budgeting:** Setting and monitoring spending limits across various categories.
- **Goal Tracking:** Defining financial objectives and tracking progress.
- **Loan & Subscription Management:** Keeping track of recurring expenses, debts, and repayment plans.
- **Calendar & Reports:** Visualizing financial data over time and generating insightful reports.
- **Authentication:** Secure user login and data protection.

## User Experience (UX) Goals
- **Clarity & Simplicity:** Data should be presented in an intuitive, easy-to-understand format.
- **Speed & Efficiency:** Fast data entry for transactions to encourage daily use.
- **Reliability:** Accurate and consistent financial tracking.
