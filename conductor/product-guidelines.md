# Product Guidelines

## 1. Design & Branding
- **Visual Aesthetic:** Clean, modern, and uncluttered. Use a neutral color palette with distinct accent colors for positive (e.g., income, goal achieved) and negative (e.g., expenses, debt) indicators.
- **UI Components:** Utilize PrimeVue components consistently across the application to maintain a unified look and feel, styled via Tailwind CSS.
- **Typography:** Highly readable sans-serif fonts to ensure clarity of numbers and financial data.

## 2. User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Frictionless Entry:** Entering transactions and adding data must require the absolute minimum number of clicks.
- **Data Visualization:** Use charts and graphs to summarize complex data visually.
- **Mobile-First Responsiveness:** The application must work seamlessly on mobile devices, ensuring users can log transactions on the go.
- **Feedback & Validation:** Clear success/error messages for all user actions.

## 3. Tone & Voice
- **Professional yet Approachable:** The language should be clear, encouraging, and free of overly complex financial jargon where possible.
- **Helpful & Reassuring:** Emphasize progress toward goals rather than punitive messaging for overspending.
