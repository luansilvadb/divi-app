# Product Guidelines: divi

## Prose Style
**Tone:** Professional, yet accessible and encouraging.
**Voice:** Second person ("You," "Your"). Focus on user empowerment.
**Consistency:** Use clear, jargon-free language to explain complex financial concepts.
**Action-Oriented:** Use active verbs to guide users through tasks (e.g., "Add," "Create," "Track").

## Branding & Visual Identity
**Design Aesthetic:** Apple-inspired Human Interface Guidelines (HIG), featuring 'Squircle' continuous curvature, sophisticated Glassmorphism (materials and vibrancy), and deep, diffuse shadows for a premium, platform-native feel.
**Color Palette:** Native Apple System Colors (e.g., SystemBlue, SystemGreen, SystemRed) utilizing Tinted and Filled variations for clear hierarchy and tactile feedback.
**Typography:** SF Pro-inspired system font stack with specific optical tracking (letter-spacing) and weights to ensure maximum legibility and elegance.
**Logo & Iconography:** Minimalist and intuitive symbols representing growth, balance, and security.

## UX Principles
**Mobile-First Design:** Ensure a seamless and responsive experience on smartphones and tablets.
**Simplicity & Clarity:** Avoid clutter. Focus on essential information for quick decision-making.
**Intuitive Navigation:** Use common design patterns and clear labels for easy discovery.
**Data Visualization:** Leverage charts and graphs to make financial data easy to understand at a glance.
**Personalization:** Provide tools for users to customize their dashboard, budgets, and goals to match their unique financial situations.
**Feedback & Affirmation:** Celebrate small wins (e.g., reaching a savings goal) and provide helpful nudges when budgets are exceeded.
**Privacy & Security:** Be transparent about data handling and provide clear indicators of a secure environment.
