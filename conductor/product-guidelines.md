# Product Guidelines: divi

## Prose Style
**Tone:** Professional, yet accessible and encouraging.
**Voice:** Second person ("You," "Your"). Focus on user empowerment.
**Consistency:** Use clear, jargon-free language to explain complex financial concepts.
**Action-Oriented:** Use active verbs to guide users through tasks (e.g., "Add," "Create," "Track").

## Branding & Visual Identity
**Design Aesthetic:** Apple-inspired HIG, Squircle curvature, Glassmorphism, and diffuse shadows for a premium, native feel.
**Color Palette:** Native Apple System Colors (Blue, Green, Red) with Tinted/Filled variations for hierarchy and tactile feedback.
**Typography:** SF Pro-inspired stack with optimized tracking and weights for legibility and elegance.
**Logo & Iconography:** Minimalist symbols for growth, balance, and security.

## UX Principles
**Mobile-First Design:** Responsive experience across all devices.
**Simplicity & Clarity:** Avoid clutter. Focus on essential information for efficient decision-making.
**Intuitive Navigation:** Standard patterns and clear labels for easy discovery.
**Data Visualization:** Clear charts for instant understanding of financial status.
**Personalization:** Tools to customize dashboards according to financial needs.
**Feedback & Affirmation:** Celebrate progress and provide helpful budget alerts.
**Privacy & Security:** Transparent data handling and secure environment indicators.
